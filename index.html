Who am I?

Hello! I go by Kene. (Think Penne with a K, so if you're suddenly hungry, blame my parents.)

The rest of this site will give you the breakdown of my professional history, but here is who I am at my core: someone who loves understanding people and solving meaningful problems.

The most rewarding moments of my career have come from sitting down with customers and cross-functional teammates, listening to their frustrations, uncovering the actual root causes, and building solutions that make their lives noticeably easier. That innate curiosity and empathy are what ultimately led me into product management.

What drives me:

  • User-First Mentality: The best products are built by people who care deeply about the users they serve.
  • Continuous Improvement: Every conversation is a chance to learn; every problem is a chance to optimize.

When I'm offline:

You'll usually find me following Chelsea FC, talking about cars, discovering new music, or enjoying the beauty nature has to offer. Whether I'm reverse-engineering how a car engine works or failing spectacularly at a pottery wheel, I just love learning how things are put together, and how to make them work even better.
